iCompete Natural Europe

iCompete Natural Europe is official partner of world class federation of natural bodybuilding with 84 Countries and growing, plus tens of thousands of members worldwide. When you become an ICN member, you're helping ICN to develop and advance your sport.

iCompeteNatural Europe (ICN EU) is official partner of world-wide fitness and natural bodybuilding organisation iCompete Natural.

“Serve and protect the Natural athlete”
- iCompete Natural motto

ICN is a highly professional organisation with the drug-free competitors. We have developed and created unprecedented technology that makes competition easier, hassle-free, fair and secure.

We try to do that while providing every competitor with a world-class and unique experience so you will remember and cherish your time with us.

European branch inherits all tradition of iCompete Natural Australia as world-class natural bodybuilding competition and brings the best experience for their members and athletes. ICN Europe has been led by Thomas Visentin for many years.

Members of Europe

ICN Europe separates member countries to three categories based on functional structure and activity.

  1. Group 1: Active member with functional structure and president
  2. Group 2: Active athletes with abroad promoters
  3. Group 3: Open to participate
