About

iCompeteNatural Europe is official partner of world class federation of natural bodybuilding with 84 Countries and growing, plus tens of thousands of members worldwide. When you become an ICN member, you're helping ICN to develop and advance your sport.

iCompeteNatural Europe (ICN EU) is official partner of world-wide fitness and natural bodybuilding organisation iCompeteNatural.

aaaa

ICN is a highly professional organisation with the drug-free competitors. We have developed and created unprecedented technology that makes competition easier, hassle-free, fair and secure.

Our motto is “Serve and protect the Natural athlete”. We try to do that while providing every competitor with a world-class and unique experience so you will remember and cherish your time with us.

The communications age and natural bodybuilding will march hand in hand into the future, the ICN is now the ultimate competition experience and will only grow stronger as time goes by. Our menu system is state of the art with a handy drill-down feature, on both sidebars.

All of the ICN Rules are fully explained, the Judging criteria for each type of division and our stringent Drug Testing rules and policy are clearly spelled out in plain English. You can conveniently join, enter online for a quality, hassle-free experience.
